How to Embed a YouTube Video in HTML

STEP 1: Share Video

Go to the Youtube video you wish to embed, and click share.

STEP 2: Click Embed

Select the Embed button.

STEP 3: Copy Embed

Copy the text shown on screen.

STEP 4: Paste Embed

Open your .html file, and paste the embed code in the body tag.

STEP 5: Create a Div

Create a div with a custom class, containing the embed code you just pasted.

STEP 6: Create a Stylesheet to format your video

Make a new .css stylesheet, and put all formatting for the video in the class you created for the div.
